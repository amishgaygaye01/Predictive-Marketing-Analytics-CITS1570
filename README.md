    <h2>About the Product</h2>
    <p>

    <h2>What is Predictive Marketing Analytics?</h2>
    <p>
        Predictive Marketing Analytics is the process of analyzing customer behavior and market trends
        to predict future outcomes. It helps businesses understand what customers are likely to buy
        and which marketing strategies can deliver better results.
    </p>

    <h2>Understanding Customer Preferences</h2>
    <p>Customers usually prefer wireless earbuds that offer:</p>
    <ul>
        <li>Good sound quality</li>
        <li>Long battery life</li>
        <li>Comfortable design</li>
        <li>Affordable pricing</li>
        <li>Fast charging features</li>
    </ul>
    <p>
        These factors play an important role in influencing purchase decisions.
    </p>

    <p>

    </p>
